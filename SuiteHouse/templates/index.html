Welcome to Suite House.

Suite House is a collection of programs to help you manage your home. Whether it be finances, chores to be done around the house, new roommates moving in, or even choosing what recipe you should have for dinner -Suite House has you covered.

Manage your Finances

  • Finance Applications

    Use our great financial applications to better your budget and yourself

    • Check Book

      Your checkbook let's you keep track of any income you have as well as variable expenses like dinner outings, food purchases, and your day to day expenses

    • Wish List

      Do you really want that new dress or new suit? Well throw it on your wish list and the Budget program will tell you if you can afford to buy it!

    • Bill Tracker

      Fixed expenses are a fact of life, keep track of your rent, electricy and other fixed expenses here.

    • Budget Assistance

      Not sure if you can afford your style of living? Want to see if you can really afford buying that new car? With the budget assistance software you can find out if you can! Simply enter all the information into your checkbook, wish list, and bill tracker, and see what you can afford!
